Invite Contacts

Go to an event of yours, click the button to open the windows for sharing the event to your contacts.
When you can see your contacts list, then click Start.
Wait until your reach the contacts limit, and the script will stop automatically. If you want to stop the process prematurely, click Stop.
If the plugin exceed the number of allowed invitations, you may have to uncheck the exceeding contacts before being able to submit the invitations.
